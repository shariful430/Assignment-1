The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Dr. Muhammad Yunus is a Bangladeshi social entrepreneur and economist, renowned for his pioneering work in microfinance and poverty alleviation. He founded Grameen Bank in 1983, which offers small loans to the impoverished without requiring collateral, empowering millions to escape poverty. Dr. Yunus’s innovative approach to banking has been recognized globally, earning him the Nobel Peace Prize in 2006. His philosophy revolves around social business, a model aimed at addressing societal issues rather than generating profit. Beyond banking, Yunus advocates for sustainable development and human rights, earning widespread respect for his efforts to create a more equitable world through financial inclusion."
